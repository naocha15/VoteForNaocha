My Agendas

Increasing the Number of Books in the College Library

This initiative focuses on expanding the college library's book collection, providing students with a broader range of essential textbooks and references to support research, assignments, and a culture of continuous learning.

Establishing a Team for Transparency

This initiative centers on the creation of a coordinating committee comprising student representatives from various semesters and subjects. Its main objective is to facilitate transparent communication by conveying student concerns to the college administration and ensuring the effective dissemination of crucial college decisions and policies.

Enhancing the Overall Infrastructure of the College

With a focus on improving the college's overall infrastructure, this initiative aims to create a more conducive learning environment. By enhancing physical facilities, safety measures, and accessibility, it seeks to provide students with an improved and enriched college experience.
